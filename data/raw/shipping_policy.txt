REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

General Refund Terms
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products purchased through our platform. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date.

Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for a refund:
- The product must be returned in its original condition
- The refund request must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
- Proof of purchase (receipt or order number) must be provided
- Digital products must not have been downloaded more than twice

Non-Refundable Items
The following items are not eligible for refunds:
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software after activation
- Custom-made or personalized items
- Sale items marked as "final sale"
- Services that have been fully rendered

Refund Processing Time
Once we receive and inspect your return:
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- The refund will be credited to your original payment method
- It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account

Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be granted in the following situations:
- Items showing signs of use or wear
- Items missing original packaging
- Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days (50% refund)

Shipping Costs
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable
- Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer
- If the return is due to our error, we will cover return shipping costs
